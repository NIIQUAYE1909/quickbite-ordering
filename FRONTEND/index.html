Support Center

Choose the fastest way to get help.

Refund Policy

We want every order to arrive correctly and on time.

If your order arrives damaged, incomplete, or significantly delayed, contact support as soon as possible with your order ID and item code.

Eligible cases may receive a replacement, store credit, or refund after review. Approved refunds are sent back to the original payment method where possible.

Concerns are reviewed case by case, so the fastest route is the in-app concern form or the support email in the footer.

Privacy Policy

QuickBite uses your information only to run and improve your orders.

We store details like your name, email, phone number, delivery address, and order history so we can process payments, deliver food, and send updates.

Driver tracking and delivery notes are used only for active deliveries and customer support.

We do not ask for more information than needed to complete your order. If you want help with your data, contact support through the email listed below.
